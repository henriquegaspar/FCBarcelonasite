Dani Alves has vowed to walk away from the game once he has ending his playing career, claiming that football is 'becoming more and more prostituted'.

The Barcelona defender, who attracted the attention of Manchester United last summer, has set his sights on another treble as part of Luis Enrique's side with the La Liga giants eight points clear at the top, well-placed to advance in the Champions League and in the Copa del Rey final.

But Alves has become disenchanted with the commercialisation of the sport in recent years, with the experienced full-back revealing he has no intention to pass on his wisdom to younger generations once he has called it a day.
