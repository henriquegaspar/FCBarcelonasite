Barcelona reportedly plan summer move for Leicester City sensation Riyad Mahrez

It is undeniable that Barcelona continue to look for a fourth attacking player to serve as a quality backup to the 'MSN' trio. And the Blaugrana could be ready to sign a Premier League sensation.

According to an "exclusive" report from the Daily Mirror, the Catalan giants are set to make a summer offer to Leicester City for Premier League sensation Riyad Mahrez, one of the best players in England this season. Mahrez has formed a fantastic duo with league's top scorer Jamie Vardy, and both have attracted interest from plenty of European giants.
